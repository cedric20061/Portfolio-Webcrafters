List of our projects

Project 1: Oriol's Shoe On deploying

We developed an online store for a shoe retailer, offering a seamless product management system without needing to handle stock. The website was designed to provide a smooth user experience with a focus on:

  • Product and discount management.
  • Order and delivery management.
  • Trends section for popular items.

Project 2: Anis Shop On deploying

Our second project involved a more complex online store, offering a wide range of products. The client sourced items from abroad, which required:

  • Stock management with product updates.
  • Highlighting products and organizing them into categories.
  • User-friendly shopping cart functionality.
  • Secure online payment methods, including FedaPaye and payment on delivery options.
  • Administrator feature for adding stories.
